\# Code of Conduct



\## Our pledge

We want this project to be a harassment-free, welcoming place for everyone who contributes or participates.



We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, inclusive, and healthy community.



\## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

\- Being respectful, kind, and professional

\- Giving constructive feedback and receiving it gracefully

\- Staying focused on technical issues and outcomes

\- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences

\- Being mindful of tone in written communication



Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

\- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech

\- Personal attacks, insults, or sustained hostility

\- Doxxing or sharing private information

\- Sexual attention or advances in project spaces

\- Trolling, intimidation, or encouraging others to harass



\## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces (issues, PRs, discussions, reviews) and in public spaces when an individual is representing the project or its community.



\## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards.



If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it privately:

\- Use GitHub’s “Report content” tools where available, and/or

\- Email: security@whispr.dev (goes to the maintainer)



We will review and investigate reports as promptly and fairly as possible.
